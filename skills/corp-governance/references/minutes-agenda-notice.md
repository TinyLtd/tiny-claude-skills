# Minutes, Agenda & Notice Templates — Exact House Format

Match these skeletons and the recurring standard phrases exactly. The single most important
process point is below.

## ⭐ Process model: minutes are NARRATIVE; approvals move to written resolution

The board meeting is for discussion and director deliberation. Substantive approvals
(financial statements, MD&A, press releases, NCIB implementation, plans, policies) are
**not** passed as motions in the minutes. Instead, the minutes record that the matter was
discussed and that **"Management was asked to circulate a written resolution of the Board
for approval."** The actual approval then happens by a separate written resolution (see
`resolution-templates.md`).

Consequently:
- **Do NOT** write `RESOLVED THAT … Carried unanimously` into discussion items in the minutes.
- **Do** record deliberation narratively and note that a written resolution will follow.
- The agenda flags these items in a **Consent Agenda** ("pre/post-approved by written
  resolution via email").
- Time-sensitive disclosure items get same-day signature: the chair script asks directors
  to sign the written resolution **the day of the meeting** so documents can be disclosed
  publicly before market open the next day.

When advising on restructuring meetings: items requiring genuine director deliberation to
satisfy directors' duties stay in-session; routine confirmations move to written resolution.

## 1. Minutes skeleton

```
[COMPANY NAME]
(the "Company")

MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE DIRECTORS OF THE COMPANY (THE "BOARD") HELD VIRTUALLY VIA
VIDEOCONFERENCE ON [WEEKDAY], [MONTH] [DAY], [YEAR], AT [TIME] [TIMEZONE].

DIRECTORS PARTICIPATING:   [Name], Chair
                           [Name]
                           [Name]
                           [Name]
                           [Name]

ALSO PARTICIPATING:        [Name], [Company]
                           [Name], [Company]
                           [Name], [external advisor / governance firm]

The following agenda materials distributed to the Board members on [DATE] are hereby
incorporated by reference:
1. [Material]
2. [Material]
   …

1. CHAIR AND SECRETARY
With the consent of the meeting, Mr./Ms. [Name] acted as Chair and Mr./Ms. [Name] acted as
Recording Secretary[ with support from Mr./Ms. [Name]].

2. NOTICE OF MEETING AND QUORUM
The Chair advised that as all of the directors were present, the meeting was duly constituted
for the transaction of business.

3. MEETING AGENDA
The Chair reviewed the Board meeting agenda and there were no comments or additions.

4. [BUSINESS ITEM HEADING — ALL CAPS]
[Narrative: who presented, what was discussed, what the directors considered, and the
outcome. For approval items: "… the directors … agreed to proceed on the proposed terms.
Management was asked to circulate a written resolution of the Board for approval."]

[ADDITIONAL BUSINESS ITEMS …]

[N]. OTHER BUSINESS
The Chair asked if there was any additional business; there was none.
[Optional: "Mr./Ms. [Name] indicated he/she would arrange for circulation of written
resolutions to approve [items]."]

[N+1]. IN-CAMERA
The Board met in camera at [TIME]; the other attendees left the meeting.

[N+2]. TERMINATION OF MEETING
There being no further business the meeting was concluded.


_______________________________            _______________________________
[CHAIR NAME — ALL CAPS]                     [SECRETARY NAME — ALL CAPS]
Chair of the Meeting                        Corporate Secretary

Confirmed: By written resolution dated [DATE]
```

The `Confirmed: By written resolution dated [DATE]` line is added once the minutes are
approved by a later written resolution. A **draft** omits the date (just `Confirmed:`).

## 2. Agenda skeleton (includes Consent Agenda + embedded chair script)

```
BOARD OF DIRECTORS MEETING AGENDA   [WEEKDAY], [MONTH] [DAY], [YEAR], [TIME] [TIMEZONE]
VIRTUAL:  [Zoom Link]

DIRECTORS:   [Name], Chair
             [Name], Vice Chair
             [Name]
             [Name]
             [Name]

GUESTS:      [Name], CEO
             [Name], CFO and Corporate Secretary
             [Name], [governance firm]

| ITEM | PRESENTER | ACTION | DURATION |
|------|-----------|--------|----------|
| [Item] | [Initials] | Review | [n] mins. |
| …      |            |        |           |
| In camera | [Chair initials] | Approve | [n] mins |

Consent Agenda (pre/post-approved by written resolution via email):
- Previous meeting minutes
- [Financials]
- [Press Release]
- [MD&A]
- [Approvals to be handled by written resolution]


BOARD MEETING SCRIPT

[Italicized items are for reference only]

| AGENDA ITEM | REMARKS FROM THE CHAIR |
|-------------|------------------------|
| [ITEM] | [Chair remarks / handoff cue] |
| … | "A written resolution to approve the financial statements, MD&A and press release
       will be circulated for signature. Please ensure that it is signed today so that the
       documents may be disclosed publicly tomorrow before market open." |
| CONCLUSION OF MEETING | "Since there is no further business to discuss, I declare the
       meeting concluded." |
```

## 3. Notice of meeting skeleton

```
TAKE NOTICE that a meeting of the Board of Directors of [COMPANY NAME] will be held by
videoconference based in [City], [Province] on [WEEKDAY], [MONTH] [DAY], [YEAR] at [TIME]
([TIMEZONE]) for the purposes of:

| ITEM | PRESENTER | ACTION | DURATION |
|------|-----------|--------|----------|
| [Item] | [Initials] | [Review/Approve] | [n] mins. |
| …      |            |          |          |

Dated this [Xth] day of [Month], [Year]


[COMPANY NAME]

 "[Name]"________________
[Name],
[Title — e.g. Chief Financial Officer and Corporate Secretary]
```

The notice and the agenda share the same item/presenter/action/duration table. The notice is
signed by the Corporate Secretary; the quoted-name convention `"[Name]"` denotes the
conformed signature.

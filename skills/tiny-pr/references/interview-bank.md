# Interview Bank — Questions to Fill the Gaps

Ask only what's missing for the identified release type. Batch related questions. Prefer
concise multiple-choice questions where there's a sensible default.

## Always (every release)
- Release type? (earnings / material change-M&A / financing / governance-AGM / company update)
- Is this **material** (could reasonably move the share price)? (drives MCR + timing rules)
- Target send date/time, and is it inside market hours (09:30–16:00 ET)?
- Who is the named spokesperson for the quote? (CEO Andrew Wilkinson / CFO / other authorized)
- Is every number in here already public or filed concurrently? (if no → which aren't)
- Approval status: Disclosure Committee + CEO signed off? (earnings: board/Audit Committee?)

## Angle & emphasis (ALWAYS ask — this is what makes it persuasive, not a data dump)
Ask these as clear user questions before drafting. The drafting team writes to these answers.
- **What should we PLAY UP?** Which 1-2 facts get the headline + first paragraph + the quote?
  (Offer the candidate facts as options; let the user pick.)
- **Headline angle?** What's the one-line story — the takeaway a reader should leave with?
- **Audience priority?** Retail holders / institutional investors / media-and-trade. (Shifts tone +
  what to lead with.)
- **What to DE-EMPHASIZE or omit?** Anything to keep in the body but not foreground, or leave out.
- **Quotes:** who speaks, and either (a) the themes you want, or (b) "draft me 2-3 options" — then
  present quote options to choose. (A quote frames *why it matters*; it must not introduce a new
  material fact found nowhere else in the release.)
- **Promotional dial:** how assertive — straight-and-factual / confident / leaning-promotional?
  (The investor-psychology agent calibrates; securities rules still cap it.)

## Earnings
- Period (e.g., Q2 2026) and currency (CAD/USD).
- Headline metrics: revenue, gross profit, Adjusted EBITDA (+ definition/reconciliation), net income,
  cash / free cash flow, recurring-revenue %, segment splits if disclosed.
- Prior-period comparatives.
- Are the FS + MD&A filed/being filed concurrently on SEDAR+? (BLOCK if not)
- Any one-time items, impairments, or restatements to flag?
- Optional CEO/CFO commentary themes for the quote.

## Material change / M&A
- What happened, in one sentence (acquisition, disposition, JV, leadership change, etc.).
- Counterparty, structure, consideration (cash/shares/earnout), % acquired, currency.
- Key multiples or metrics being disclosed (only if public/permitted).
- Conditions & approvals (TSX, shareholder, lender, regulatory) and expected close timing.
- Related-party? (triggers MI 61-101 — special committee, valuation, minority approval)
- MCR plan (SEDAR+ within 10 days).

## Financing
- Instrument (equity, debt, convertible, NCIB, refinancing), size, price, currency.
- Underwriters/agents; brokered vs non-brokered; bought deal?
- Use of proceeds (factual).
- TSX conditional approval / pre-clearance status. (often required BEFORE release)
- Closing timing; any selling restrictions / legends (US/Reg S).

## Governance / AGM
- Event (director appointment/departure, AGM voting results, dividend declaration, auditor change).
- For voting results: each resolution + for/against/withheld.
- For dividend: amount, record date, payment date, eligible-dividend designation.
- Effective dates and any board/committee context.

## Company update (non-material)
- The news (product, partnership, milestone, hire, op update).
- Confirm it is genuinely **non-material** (if it might be material, route through the material path).
- Any metrics quoted — are they public?
- Note: non-material updates still post wire-first per policy; don't let social precede the wire.
